Branch metrics are calculated using two different algorithms:

Leaf stories
Done count The sum of the all done leaf stories' story points inside the current story. Leaf stories are stories that do not have children.
Total count The sum of all leaf stories' story points
Estimated progress
Estimated progress is calculated for each story in the branch and their children recursively. The story's own story point estimate is compared to the sum of its children story points. The greater number will be included in the estimate.
