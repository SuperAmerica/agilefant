When timesheets are enabled, you can log spent effort to tasks, stories, iterations, projects or products.


Opening the spent effort dialog of a backlog.
Double clicking the ES cell opens the same dialog as the spent effort link in the edit menu.

When timesheets are enabled, the Timesheets tab appears. On this tab you can generate reports of spent effort and export the information as an Excel document.

Note! Logging spent effort does not affect the estimated effort left for a task. See the forum for an explanation.
